The most awaited University Days 2023 happened last March 8 to March 11, 2023. This is the first ever celebration of U-Days since the pandemic hit. There were spectacular events, stalls, rides, and fantastic performances that the students prepared for months. The following events are further elaborated below.

UNIVERSITY DAYS PROPER

HIGHLIGHTS

RIDES & GAMES

Thriliing rides were located beside the IH Gymansium.

STALLS

Food stalls, school organizations, and stores were located all over the campus.

MAIN EVENTS

Events like sports, street dance, dance competition, Mr. and Ms. HAU, and concert were the most awaited program by the students and visitors of Holy Angel University.

UNIVERSITY DAYS

DAY 1 HIGHLIGHTS

The first day of the university days was the most awaited day to witness the gathering of different students from different schools. For the first time after three years, the university parade was held at the school boulevard, and we have witnessed the collective efforts the students showcased in making their props and proving the true essence of angelites core value in excellence.

The academic and non-academic events during the first half of the first day are mobile legends finals, debate competition, volleyball finals, call of duty finals. Meanwhile, this was followed by the opening salvo, street dance competition, and the pre-pageant of Mr. and Ms. HAU 2023.

Evidently, the school was filled with the presence of students from different places and institutions. People were seen queuing patiently for the rides and food stalls.

09
Mar
UNIVERSITY DAYS

DAY 2 HIGHLIGHTS

The dance competition or known as BDC finals was held during the second day of the event. This showcased the amazing talent and flexibility of the angelites when it comes to grooving. Apart from that, the professors also revealed their hidden talent besides from teaching. Also, BINI, a ppop girl group, also appeared as the special and surprise guest of the night.

The finals for the basketball competition and e-sport event valorant were held during this day. The teams coming to finals in basketball were SEA vs SBA. SEA ordered the court bringing SBA comnig home to a second. On the other hand, in the valorant competition, the teams fighting for the championship were SEA vs SOC. Again, SEA continues to dominate both the sports and e-sports department.

DAY 3 HIGHLIGHTS

10

Last Day of Competitions.

The third day is the final day for competitions. Whereas, laro ng lahi, ted talk competition, kanto boys and girls, and the Mr. and Ms. HAU 2023 coronation night were held. The stunning angelites who participated in the Mr. and Ms. HAU 2023 made the night of the students even brigther with their beautiful costumes and formal wear. With the conclusion of the event, the titled Mr. HAU was from the school of business and accountancy while the winner of Ms. HAU was from SNAMS (the only candidate who joined without a partner).

UNIVERSITY DAYS

DAY 4 HIGHLIGHTS

11
Mar

CONCERT

The last day of the University Days served as one of the most memorable nights for some students. Mainstream artists like Toneejay, and Orange and Lemons performed on the later part of the program.

While most angelites and outsiders enjoy the free concert inside the campus, there were still majority of students who were not able to enter beacuse of the crowd control done by the security. This brought chaos and rallies outside the campus and a lot of students were dismayed for queuing so long outside.

Nevertheless, I beleive that most of the students enjoyed the University Days since it is by far one of the most remarkable event done by the USSC ever since the pandemic came. This marks a new beginning for the Holy Angel University community since socialization is being acknowledged again.
